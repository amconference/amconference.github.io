Posters

We would like to invite anyone who wishes to share their experience in the altmetrics or research communication space to bring along a poster showcasing their experience. This is intended to be very informal; the idea is to get people talking and share ideas.

Perhaps you have...

  • Run a workshop to encourage your faculty to share their research online?
  • Worked with the communications office to highlight a particular piece of work?
  • Included altmetrics data in your marketing campaigns?

We’d love to hear about it all! Posters will be displayed in the refreshment area for viewing during breaks and the drinks reception, with a £100 prize offered for the best one!

If you’d like to bring one along please fill in the form below. The winner will be chosen by a judging panel on the Thursday evening. (Only those submitted from non-commercial organisations are eligible for the prize - but publishers are still very welcome to display.)
