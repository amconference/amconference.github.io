Travel grants

A limited number of travel grants are available to librarians and researchers who wish to attend the conference. To apply for a grant, please email your name, job title, affiliation, and answers to the questions below to altmetricsconf@gmail.com

Applications will close on Friday the 29th of August, and the selected recipients informed the following week.

  1. Describe your interest in altmetrics and its potential use and value for your field.
  2. Describe your contributions to date in the altmetrics conversation (sharing with colleagues, research on altmetrics, attendance in altmetrics conference/workshops, etc.).
  3. Please provide an estimated budget for your conference travel expenses.
