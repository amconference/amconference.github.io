Travel grants


Thanks to our generous sponsors, we are pleased to offer travel grants for academics and institutional representatives for this year's conference. Funds may be used to cover conference registration fees, transport, and accommodation.

To request a travel grant, please complete the application form so that we can get to know more about your interests and contributions in the development of altmetrics, particularly in the past 12 months. Past grant awardees are eligible to apply if they have demonstrated continued service in this arena.

Completed forms must be submitted by 5pm BST on 31 July. The conference committee will notify all applicants by 31 August of the application decision.
